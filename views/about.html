Purpose: To manage your diet!

The purpose of CalorieBuddy is to help people manage their diet by displaying nutritional facts including calories, carbs, fat, protein, salt and sugar in a recipe based on food ingredients in the recipe.

Description: A user-friendly Design.

CalorieBuddy functions as a digital calorie counter to help you manage your diet.

Essentially it interacts with you to calculate and display nutritional facts including calories for your recipes or meals based on food ingredients in the recipe or the meal.

You are given the choice to add nutritional facts for food ingredients. It also provides you with the sum of the calories for a recipe or a meal based on the food ingredients selected by the you to be included in the recipe or the meal.

If a food ingredient is not in the database, you are given the choice to add it to our database.
