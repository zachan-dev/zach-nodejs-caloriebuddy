Add

You can add a new food item to the app. You will have to provide the following information of that food: name, typical values, unit of the typical value, calories, carbs, fat, protein, salt, and sugar.

Search

Once you added the food item, you can then search by food name. The food information related to the search will be displayed.

Update

You can change any of the food information at any time. Simply search by food name and update it. You can also delete the whole food item if you need to.
